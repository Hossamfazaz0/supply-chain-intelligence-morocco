## 🏭 Business Problem

A fictional Label'Vie-style FMCG distributor operating across Morocco faces a critical logistics challenge:

- **8 warehouses** across major Moroccan cities
- **20 retail stores** spread nationwide
- **Current approach:** manual allocation based on gut feeling and simple rules
- **Current cost:** 38M MAD per year in transport + holding costs
- **Core question:** which warehouse should supply which store, how much, and when — to minimize total cost while respecting capacity and delivery constraints?
